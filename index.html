A free, language-agnostic, exercise of will.

Hi, I'm Ben Orenstein!

I've been teaching people how to improve their code for six years.

In that time, I've seen again and again that a steady drum-beat of small wins beats occasional large efforts every time.

Let's commit to 30 days of tiny wins

Every work day (Mon-Fri), I'll send the group a small assignment that will improve the quality of the codebase you work on.

Your job is to implement the change, and report your experience back to the group. We'll support each other through the challenges, and celebrate our victories together.

Details:

  • The course is free.
  • The assignments are language-agnostic.
  • Each assignment will take ~20 minutes.

Is this for you?

Yes, if...

  • you've worked on a Rails app long enough to see development slow down.
  • shipping features faster is a priority.
  • you want specific, tactical information on refactorings you can implement.
